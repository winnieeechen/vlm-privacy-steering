Qwen2.5-VL-7B Balanced CATS
===========================

This applies method 10 to Qwen/Qwen2.5-VL-7B-Instruct.

For every train image, the model is teacher-forced with A, B, and C answer
suffixes. Over and under transitions are constructed from true_label rather
than the model's naturally occurring case_type distribution.

The default aggregation is balanced_mean. Over and under extraction share one
suffix-hidden-state cache. Because the original case-based 7B mean vectors are
not currently available, vectors are rescaled to the layer norm of the raw
mean transition delta.

Default evaluation setting:

layer = 32
alpha = 0.5
k_answer_tokens = 8
