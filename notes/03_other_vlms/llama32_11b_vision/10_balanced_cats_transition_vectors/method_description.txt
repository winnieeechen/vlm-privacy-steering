10 Balanced CATS transition vectors
====================================

Data source:
    Every Llama training sample with true_label A/B/C, independent of the
    model's naturally occurring case_type.

For true A, over transitions are B->A and C->A.
For true B, over transition is C->B and under transition is A->B.
For true C, under transitions are A->C and B->C.

Each transition is:
    delta = h(true answer suffix) - h(counterfactual wrong answer suffix)

Transition types are averaged separately and then averaged with equal weight.
The selected balanced-mean vector is rescaled to the method-02 mean-vector norm.

The payload also stores sign_aligned_pc1 for method 10b. Only behavior vectors
change; method-02 condition vectors and gates are reused.
