06 CATS-PCA behavior vectors
============================

Data source:
    Natural over/under errors in the Llama train predictions.

For each natural error:
    delta = h(true answer suffix) - h(base-predicted wrong answer suffix)

Each h is the mean hidden state over the first K=8 teacher-forced answer
tokens. Per layer, deltas are unit-normalized and centered; sign-aligned PC1 is
selected and rescaled to the corresponding method-02 mean-vector norm.

Only behavior vectors change. Method-02 condition vectors and gates are reused.
