Observation
============================================================

The combined router is better than either standalone safe final correction on test:
- over-only final conditional: Correct +1, Over -1, Under 0
- under-only final conditional: Correct +3, Over 0, Under -3
- combined router: Correct +4, Over -1, Under -3

The combined result is still modest, mainly because the over behavior vector is weak for Llama.
The under vector contributes most of the utility gain, while the over route gives a small over-disclosure reduction.
