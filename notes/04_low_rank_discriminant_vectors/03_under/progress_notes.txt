Conclusion

Compared with the base model:
Correct +4
Over-disclosure -3
Under-disclosure -1

Compared with the original safe under conditional setting:

Over-disclosure -3

This is the stronger of the two low-rank discriminant results so far. Unlike the
over fisher_pca setting, it may be worth carrying to test.


Recommended next checks
-----------------------
1. Run test for the current fisher_pca under setting, using the validation-best

2. Run a tighter threshold sweep around the actual under score scale:
   -0.0125, -0.010, -0.0075, -0.005, -0.0025, 0.000,
    0.0025, 0.005, 0.0075, 0.010, 0.0125

3. Reuse the cached activations to test ensemble and pca_residual variants.
