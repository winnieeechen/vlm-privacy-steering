Conclusion
----------

Balanced CATS shows a real but limited steering signal. Layer 28 is better than
layers 32 and 34 for this answer-suffix vector, and alpha 1.0 is better than 0.5
at layer 28. The test result confirms the val conclusion: the method improves
over the base model slightly, but remains clearly below 02 mean. It should not
replace 02 mean as the primary Qwen-3B method. It may still be useful as a
model-agnostic fallback when another model has missing natural case types.
